About Me

A bit more about my background, how I think about software engineering, and what motivates the work I do.

Biography

I’m Nkosilathi Sibanda, a diligent and enthusiastic software engineer with a strong foundation in computer science and practical experience across AI, cloud, and full‑stack development. I hold an MSc in Computer Science from Nottingham Trent University (Distinction) and a BSc (Hons) in Computer Science from Coventry University, where I deepened my understanding of algorithms, software engineering, and systems design through a series of demanding projects.

Professionally, I have worked at Accenture in roles spanning packaged app development, UI development, and Gen AI engineering. I’ve contributed to projects such as a digital AI assistant in the mortgage sector, agentic solutions for finance operations, and AI‑driven document analysis for the energy industry. Earlier in my journey I gained experience through the Digital Futures Academy and a placement as a software engineer at ENSEK, working with technologies like ASP.NET, C#, SQL, and Azure.

What motivates me is building effective, efficient, and robust applications that solve real problems and are grounded in solid engineering principles. I care about clarity, good communication, and continuous improvement—whether that’s refining a design, strengthening a codebase, or learning a new concept in AI or cloud computing!
